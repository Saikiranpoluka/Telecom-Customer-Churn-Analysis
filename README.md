# Telecom Customer Churn & Retention Analysis

## 📌 Project Objective
This project analyzes a dataset of 7,000+ telecom subscribers to construct a robust retention model. The objective is to identify high-risk customer profiles, quantify revenue loss, and propose data-driven business interventions to reduce churn.

## 📉 Key Insights & Churn Drivers
* **Financial Impact:** Quantified a 26.54% overall customer churn rate, isolating a $139,000 monthly revenue loss for the business.
* **Payment Method Risks:** Segmented user behavior and pinpointed peak churn among 'Electronic Check' users, who dropped off at a staggering rate of 45.29%.
* **Security & Support:** Identified that accounts lacking 'Online Security' experienced a massive 41.77% churn rate, making technical support a primary driver of retention.

## 🚀 Strategic Recommendations & ROI
* **Targeted Incentives:** Recommended offering months of free TechSupport or OnlineSecurity to high-risk profiles to directly address the segment churning at >41%.
* **Self-Funding Retention Model:** Demonstrated that the capital recovered by reducing overall churn by just 5% completely offsets the cost of $10 loyalty discounts, turning this strategy into a self-funding retention system.
